our story

Born in Margaret River, WA, we are one of the first companies in Australia to produce a functional beverage leveraging the benefits of Hemp Extract. Tired of consuming energy drinks that consist mostly of caffine, we sought an alternative that would be perfect for social occasions and healthier than other types of carbonated beverages.

What we found was Adaptogens, that can stimulate the nervous system to provide additional energy and focus. Not only do our drinks provide cognative benefits, they also taste great. We have partnered with local producers to design flavours that are unique to Australia, such as Honey and Euctalyptus. We are extremely proud of what we produce, and we hope that you will enjoy our products as much as we enjoy making them.
